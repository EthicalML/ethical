The 8 commitments of the ML Pledge

The 8 commitments for technology leaders towards the development of
safe, reliable, robust and industry-ready machine learning systems.

Human augmentation

I commit to develop systems that augment humans by design. My aim is to ensure how I can abstract manual end-to-end processes safely through the relevant human validation at an efficient point in the process.

Algorithmic Bias

I commit to build the infrastructure required to evaluate the bias and variance in the models & data I have available to evaluate. Furthermore I aim to make an reasonable and responsible decision based on application-specific bias risks when deploying a model to production.

Re-skilling Strategy

I commit to encourage strategic plans on how to empower and educate individuals to use our technology as specific processes are automated and new ones are created through our systems.

Pragmatic Accuracy Metrics

I commit to make explicit effort to ensure we understand the expectations from external stakeholders on the accuracy from their intuitive perspective and I aim to ensure the metrics we base our accuracy is as close and transparent as possible.

Transparency

I commit to ensure we are able to build reasonable infrastructure to make our models transparent. Whether it is audit trails of training data, explainability of weights in the models or any other method - I will make an explicit effort to ensure it's possible to provide reasonable transparency without putting in danger IP.

Scalability

I commit to understand the infastructure requirements to ensure the framework that is deployed with the models is robust, scalable and reliable. This includes having the relevant devops capability around the models to ensure they can be deployed, updated, and edited as necessary. This point encompasses uptime, backwards compatibility, abstraction of ML pipeline, and other scapability measurements.

Trust beyond the user

I commit to ensure I am able to provide methods for accountability with external stakeholders that enable me to understand and match their requirements and expectations to a reasonable extent. This involves an explicit effor to avoid making assumptions, and break down to a reasonable extent the storage, usage and purpose of data storage. This involves key stakeholders beyond just users of the product - ranging from procurement managers, legal stakeholders, executives and other individuals that may be involved.

Cyberdefense

I commit to build the internal processes required both technical and non-technical to ensure relevant stakeholders are aware of the potential cybersecurity loopholes within the system. This includes algorithmic bias, adversarial attacks, model zero-day exploits / backdoors

We bring together key stakeholders to define a set of key practical processes and actions that can be taken in the workplace for the consious and ethical development of AI.

The results from the processes that are taken forward are used to abstract best practices and frameworks that should be implemented in the workplace, which also serve as case studies and practical examples to accelerate public policy that enforces the ethical development of AI.

Accelerating policy for the Ethical Development of AI in Industry

Driving policy through results obtained from implementing best practices in AI, Data & Machine Learning in the workplace.

The Institute focuses on the biggest immediate burning platforms on AI development. This includes some of the biggest risks that come when designing, developing and deploying Machine Learning systems in industry. Our mission is to ensure we educate and empower both, machine learning suppliers and industry players to mitigate risks and make use of the full potential of AI.

The Institute continues to bring together academics, government officials and renowned executives to create, implement and advocate the most critical burning platforms in Data, AI and Machine Learning. We continuously gather results to support and accelerate efficient policy that enforces the ethical development of AI and Machine Learning.
